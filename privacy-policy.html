Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 October 2024

Welcome to JTL Aluminium Design ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy (or "Notice") explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard the personal data that you provide to us through our website jtlaluminiumdesign.my (the "Website"), or in the course of our business operations.

By accessing or using our Website, or by interacting with us in any way (e.g., requesting a quotation, contacting us), you agree to the collection and use of your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data when you interact with us:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title.
  • Transactional Data: Information related to project enquiries, quotations, orders (e.g., project specifications, billing or shipping address).
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited on our site, time spent on pages.
  • Communications: Records of your enquiries — via email, contact forms, phone calls, or chat — including any information you choose to share with us.
  • Cookies & Tracking Data: We use cookies (or similar technologies) to remember your session, preferences, and to analyze site usage.

We collect this data:

  • When you submit a contact or quotation form;
  • When you request a catalog, brochure, or sample;
  • When you sign up for newsletters or updates;
  • When you browse or interact with our Website;
  • When you communicate with us by phone, email, or other means.

2. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal data for these purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries and provide quotations, project advice, or consultation.
  • To process orders or service engagements, including billing, shipping, and project delivery.
  • To communicate with you about our products, promotions, company news, or updates (if you agree to receive such communications).
  • To improve our Website: analyze usage so we can make our Website more user-friendly and functional.
  • To comply with legal obligations or regulatory requirements in Malaysia.
  • For security purposes, for example to detect or prevent fraud or unauthorized access.

3. Use of Cookies

We use cookies on our Website to:

  • Keep track of your session (so you don't have to re-enter data during your visit)
  • Remember your preferences (e.g., language, project interests)
  • Help us analyze how our Website is being used so we can improve it

You may choose to disable or clear cookies through your browser settings. However, if you do so, certain parts of our Website (e.g., the form you filled or the quotation you were building) may not function as intended.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or alteration. These include technical, administrative, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data. Only our authorized personnel (or trusted third-party service providers) have access to your data, on a "need-to-know" basis.

5. Sharing & Disclosure of Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

However, we may disclose your personal data to:

  • Our trusted service providers (for example, for website hosting, customer relationship management, or email communications)
  • Business partners or subcontractors (e.g., for manufacturing, delivery, or installation of aluminium products)
  • Legal or regulatory authorities, if required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Other parties with your explicit consent

Any third party that receives your data is bound to maintain its confidentiality and use it only for the permitted purpose.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the relevant business purposes, such as:

  • Completing your project or order
  • Maintaining records for accounting or legal obligations
  • Marketing communications (if you have opted in)

When personal data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights Under Malaysian PDPA

Under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia, you have certain rights with respect to your personal data, including:

  • Access: Request to view the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw your consent for us to process your data for certain purposes (e.g., marketing), subject to legal or contractual constraints.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, where permitted by law.
  • Limitation: Request that we limit the processing of your data in certain ways.

To exercise your rights, please contact us (see "Contact Us" below).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time (for example, to reflect changes in our business, our Website, or legal requirements). When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you (if you are an existing contact) via email or other means where appropriate
  • Post the updated Privacy Policy on our Website

We encourage you to review this page periodically.
